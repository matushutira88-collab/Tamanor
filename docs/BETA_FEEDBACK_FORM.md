# Guardora.ai — Beta Feedback Form

Ask each tester these. Free-text unless noted; 1–5 scale where marked (1 = not
at all, 5 = completely).

## Clarity & trust

1. Is it clear what problem Guardora solves? (1–5) + why
2. Is the difference between **demo data** and **real read-only sync** clear?
   (1–5) + why
3. Does the product feel trustworthy? (1–5) + why
4. Is the **dashboard** understandable? (1–5) + what confused you
5. Is the **Inbox** understandable? (1–5) + what confused you
6. Is the **approval workflow** understandable (propose → approve → execute,
   nothing runs without a human)? (1–5) + why

## Language & visuals

7. Do the **SK** texts read naturally? (1–5) + examples to fix
8. Do the **DE** texts read naturally? (1–5) + examples to fix
9. Are the **charts and topics** clear? (1–5) + what was unclear
10. Do the **emoji / icons** help you understand sentiment/risk faster? (1–5) +
    any that feel wrong or childish

## Value & priorities

11. What would be needed **before a paid test**?
12. What would you **pay for** — and what would you **not** pay for?
13. Which **platform** matters most to you (Facebook / Instagram / YouTube /
    LinkedIn / TikTok / Google / other)? + why

## Anything else

14. Biggest single thing to improve?
15. Anything that made you distrust the product?
